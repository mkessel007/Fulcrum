Fulcrum - The customization central core plugin for WordPress

Fulcrum: a thing that plays a central or essential role in an activity, event, or situation

Fact: Websites have a lot of redundancies

Fulcrum is a centralized repository where common features and functionality are readily available for use in your project. It uses configuration-over-code to spin up new custom post types, taxonomies, widgets, shortcodes, and more. There's no need to carry a library of snippets around when you can configure what you want and let Fulcrum handle it for you.

Bottomline: Fulcrum saves you time, money, and frustrations.
